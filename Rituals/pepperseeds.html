ENTRY 01 — Ecosystem Re-Entry

The next time you buy a bell pepper, especially a red, orange or yellow one, save the seeds.
In the spring, throw them into an empty lot and let nature do its thing. You are creating magic.
An alchemist of the soil. You are creating the conditions for free food—for your future self, for the birds, and for the soil.
This is small, but not trivial.
A few minutes of your time and you will have inserted yourself back into your local ecosystem with very little effort.
